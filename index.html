Hi there! Thanks for dropping by. I am Sanam Karki, a 5X Certified Salesforce Admin.

I have been working in the Salesforce ecosystem for 3 years now. I officially started my Salesforce journey as an "Accidental Admin" in 2019. As I discovered more about the workings of Salesforce, I fell in love with it.

Over the years, my Salesforce knowledge has been enhanced by attending various Salesforce conferences, studying rigorous hours for certifications, volunteering for non-profits and meeting new Salesforce professionals in weekly Salesforce meetups. Currently, I am more focused on enhancing my programming skills. I am learning Salesforce programming language at the moment including APEX and designing Lightning Web Component (LWC).

I am passionate about helping women join Salesforce and I am constantly mentoring women who are looking to break into the Salesforce world. This mentorship might be in terms of personal guidance, Instagram content Creation or formal volunteer meetings. I also run yearly project called "CurlyCodes". This projects aims to bridge the digital divide among the students of rural areas in Nepal by providing technical(coding curriculum) as well as financial support to purchase computers.

During my free time, I love spending time with my family, go on tough hikes, play with my dog (Coffee) and record YouTube cover songs.
